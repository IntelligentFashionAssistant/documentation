   - put vs patch

** End points
   |---------------+------------------+-------------------------------+-----------------------------|
   | *Method*      | *End Points*     | *Request*                     | *Response*                  |
   |---------------+------------------+-------------------------------+-----------------------------|
   | post          | /user/register   | body(UserEntity)              | status:success              |
   | post          | /user/login      | body(email, password)         | data: token, status:success |
   | put (patch??) | /user/           | body(Userentity, UserId)      | status:success              |
   | post          | /user/profile    | body(bodySizesentity, UserId) | status:success              |
   | get           | /user/profile    | parameters(UserId)            | userentity, Bodysizesentity |
   | put           | /user/profile    | body(bodysizesentity, UserId) | status:success              |
   |---------------+------------------+-------------------------------+-----------------------------|
   | get           | /garment/:UserId | __                            | list of Garmententity       |
   | get           | /garment/:UserId | parameters(category=)         | list of Garmententity       |
   | get           | /garment/:UserId | parameters(searchkeyword=)    | list of Garmententity       |
